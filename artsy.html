WHAT IS ARTSY?

Artsy application is a wholesome community for artists. The aforementioned application has multiple catagories ranging from music to painting to photography..so on and so forth. Users have the ability to follow/unfollow other users, showcase their work or even get freelance jobs through consumers according to their preferneces.

OVERVIEW

This application has been established to bridge the gap between artists and art connoisseurs.

5Ws?

WHO?

Artists who are looking for a specific online platform dedicated to art to showcase their works.Admirers who are looking for the same specific platform to enjoy artistic outcomes of different genres.

WHAT?

Creating an application to bridge the gap between artists and art connoisseurs and provide a feature-rich platform for all kinds of artists.Building an unique and helpful network to help out deserving artists.

WHEN?

The application will be a helping hand to those who are in need of a minimal platform to showcase their art.Undervalued artists looking to showcase their works.
Art enthusiasts who are in need of a platform to explore art, get inspired and find suitable artworks to purchase.

WHERE?

Can be implemented to any region. Keeping the above mentioned point, cultural significance of a specific region is important and a key factor to generate users, a detailed research can be done to enhance the application according to that.

WHY?

To build a community for artists.Providing a platform for undervalued artists.
